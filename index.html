MFC
Multi-Component Flow Code
GitHub - Download - Documentation
Welcome to the home of MFC!

MFC simulates compressible multi-component and multi-phase flows, amongst other things. It scales ideally to tens of thousands of GPUs. MFC is a Fortran codebase that makes use of metaprogramming to keep the codebase readable and compact.
